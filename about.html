ABOUT OUR ORGANIZATION

News Grid is a nonprofit devoted to spreading news,and ideas usually in the form of short, concise articles. News Grid began in 1984 as a platform where Technology, Entertainment and Design converged, and today covers almost all topics — from science to business to global issues. Meanwhile, independently run News Grid help share ideas in communities around the world.

Our Mission: Spread ideas and news across the globe

News Grid is a global community, welcoming people from every discipline and culture who seek a deeper understanding of the world. We believe passionately in the power of ideas to change attitudes, lives and, ultimately, the world. On NewsGrid.org, we're building a clearinghouse of free knowledge from the world's most inspired thinkers — and a community of curious souls to engage with ideas and each other, both online and and through newpapers and magazines.
